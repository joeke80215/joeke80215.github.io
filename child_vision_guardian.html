5. Data Retention & Deletion

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and Taiwan’s Personal Data Protection Act, you may request access to, deletion of, or restriction on processing of diagnostic data related to your device. Contact us at jiidea0116@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not specifically directed to children under 13. Because we do not collect personal information, no additional child data protection mechanisms are required. If you believe a child has provided us with any data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Permissions & Sensitive Data

The app only requests basic network access (INTERNET) to transmit diagnostic data. It does not request location, camera, contacts, or other dangerous permissions.

9. Information Security

  • Data in transit is encrypted using TLS.
  • Only authorized developers at Jiidea Studio have access to the Firebase project, protected by two‑factor authentication.
  • We regularly update dependencies and run security scans.

10. International Data Transfers

Firebase servers may be located in multiple regions worldwide. Google provides an equivalent level of protection through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and related privacy frameworks.
